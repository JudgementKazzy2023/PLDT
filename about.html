About PLDT

PLDT is the leading telecommunications and digital services provider in the Philippines. With a strong commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction, we deliver a wide range of services including:

  • High-speed Internet
  • Reliable landline services
  • Entertainment options
  • Smart home solutions

Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to connecting people and businesses across the Philippines, enhancing lives through seamless communication and advanced digital solutions.

Our Mission

At PLDT, our mission is to provide innovative digital solutions that empower businesses and individuals to succeed in the digital age.

Our History

PLDT was founded in 1995 with a vision to bring reliable and fast internet connectivity to the Philippines. Today, we are a leading provider of digital solutions in the country.
